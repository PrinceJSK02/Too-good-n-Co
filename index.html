Why we opened

A cafe built for the people who actually live here

Too Good n Co started with a simple idea: Joondalup didn't need another big chain coffee run — it needed somewhere small enough to know your order and unhurried enough to make it properly.

We bake fresh, brew carefully, and keep the menu tight so nothing's an afterthought. The homewares on the shelf are picked the same way the coffee beans are: because we'd want them ourselves.

  • ✓ Coffee roasted and brewed with care, every single cup
  • ✓ Catering available for offices, workshops and gatherings nearby
  • ✓ A cosy yard out back when you want to sit and stay a while
